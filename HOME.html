About Information

Virtual Labs will provide students an online simulator or calculator that models the physical phenomenon set by an experiment and will thus allow them to obtain and/or observe the results of that particular experiment using the measured data provided by the students for virtual lab experiments. This would help in learning, analysing and exploring concepts, ideas and theories through distant experimentation.
