Summary


While doing the experiment you might have learned how the different parameters affect the deflection in a beam.


Deflection in a beam is directly proportion to the values of load acting on its free end and the Length of the beam i.e the higher these values get, chances of failures in beam increases


While the modulus of elasticity (different for different types of materials) and axial moment of inertia which is determined by the shape and size of cross section) are inversely proportional. Hence, by increasing these parameters one can reduce the deflection in the beam


Which is why the proper study for selection of materials and specifications for different types of application is a must in order to avoid unwanted failures
