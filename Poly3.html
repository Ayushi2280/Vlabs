Observations


The resultant force vector in first canvas is having the same size (magnitude) as that of the closing side of the polygon in the second canvas.

The direction & orientation is also the same for both of them.

The calculated magnitude and direction provided by the simulator provides justification for the above points.

Try doing some analytical calculations on paper, you will notice that the results you got from the simulator matching what you have done on paper.

Summary


The experiment gives proof for the theory. The Law of Polygon of Forces : if any number of coplanar concurrent forces can be represented in magnitude and direction by the sides of a polygon taken in order; then their resultant will be represented by the closing side of the polygon taken in opposite order.
