What is RigVIT ?

RigVIT a web based virtual lab data simulation platform made by an Interdisciplinary team of undergraduates of VIT Bhopal University


Aim & Purpose

Our aim is to provide students a safe and easy to access lab environment for them to conduct and gather analytical and numerical data for their studies. It is accessible from anywhere and through any computer or mobile devices which have the capability of internet connectivity and multimedia
